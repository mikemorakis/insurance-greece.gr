## What you need to know about the electronic transactions' lottery and how to check if you are one of the lucky winners!

The Finance Ministry conducted a draw based on taxpayers' electronic transactions via debit or credit card from January 1 to September 30, 2017. This Christmas draw distributed 1,000 Euros each to 9,000 fortunate citizens.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Who is responsible for conducting the draw?

The Independent Public Revenue Authority, part of the Finance Ministry.

### 2. Who participates in the draw?

Any citizen with a Greek AFM (tax identification number) over 18 years old is eligible.

### 3. What action is required to participate?

Complete at least one electronic transaction using a card or e-Banking payment during the draw period.

### 4. How do you prove online transactions?

You do not have to take any action. Your electronic payments are automatically converted into lottery tickets by the system.

### 5. How many tickets do you receive?

Ticket allocation based on total electronic spending:

- Up to 100€: 1 ticket per 1€

- 101€-500€: 1 ticket per 2€

- 501€-1,000€: 1 ticket per 3€

- Over 1,000€: 1 ticket per 4€

### 6. How do you check if you've won?

Visit AADE website using your Taxisnet credentials. Winners receive personalized messages through their Taxisnet account.

### 7. What is the prize?

Each winner receives 1,000 Euros in cash.
