## Six Important Tips When Buying Car Insurance

- **Financial stability:** Research the company's financial record, claim handling timeframe, and solvency margin

- **Road Assistance:** Confirm coverage extends beyond accidents to include breakdowns (flat tires, mechanical failure, etc.)

- **Green Card:** Verify whether international insurance documentation is included or requires additional fees

- **Vehicle valuation:** Ensure annual contract reviews reflect current commercial value

- **Accessibility:** Confirm 24-hour support availability when advisors are unavailable

- **Uninsured driver protection:** Verify coverage exists (notably important given over one million uninsured vehicles in Greece)

**Critical Warning:** A drunk driver is considered a driver with no insurance cover by the Greek insurance firms!
