This guide explains how to register with your family doctor in Greece, or check if you are already allocated to one.

## Requirements

To complete the registration process, you will need:

- TaxisNet credentials

- AMKA number

## How to Register

Visit the online registration portal at: [https://www.e-syntagografisi.gr/p-rv/p](https://www.e-syntagografisi.gr/p-rv/p)

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete your registration. The system will guide you through the process step by step.
