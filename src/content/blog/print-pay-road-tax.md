Need to print your Road Tax notice without Taxisnet credentials? Here's how to do it.

## How to Print Your Road Tax Notice

Visit the official Greek tax authority portal at: [https://www1.aade.gr/myaade/telh-kykloforias-without-sso/#!/mainview](https://www1.aade.gr/myaade/telh-kykloforias-without-sso/#!/mainview)

## Need Help?

If you're experiencing difficulties, you can email your AFM (Greek tax identification number) and vehicle license plate details to info@insurance-greece.com, and we will return the notice via email.
